Why is this course for women only?

There is a well documented gender diversity problem in the technology industry (link?). Sky recognises that a diverse workforce brings benefits in terms of both the quality of output and the wellbeing of employees (definitely need links for these claims?!). We therefore believe that increasing gender diversity in technology is a valuable aim.

Is there an equivalent for men?

Currently we don’t offer a beginner level course for men or mixed groups. However, there are many free courses available elsewhere open to anyone interested in starting out in technology (links). If you already have experience in technology and are interested in working for Sky, please have a look at the opportunities available on Work For Sky (link).
